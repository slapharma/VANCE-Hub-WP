Lemon Herb Salmon with Greens

🍽️ Lemon Herb Salmon with Greens

A simple, elegant salmon dinner with Mediterranean herbs, wilted spinach and lentils or brown rice.

⏱️
5 min
Prep
🔥
15 min
Cook
⌚
20 min
Total
👤
2
Serves
Omega-3 RichMediterranean-StyleHigh ProteinGluten-FreeDairy-FreeNutrient-Dense

Ingredients

  • ·2 salmon fillets
  • ·1 tbsp olive oil
  • ·Juice of 1 lemon
  • ·1 tsp dried oregano
  • ·2 cups fresh spinach
  • ·1 cup cooked lentils or brown rice
  • ·Salt and black pepper, to taste

Method

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 180°C.

  2. 2

    Place salmon on a baking tray lined with baking paper.

  3. 3

    Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice; sprinkle with oregano.

  4. 4

    Season with salt and pepper. Bake for 12–15 minutes until cooked through.

  5. 5

    Heat a small pan with olive oil. Sauté spinach for 1–2 minutes until wilted.

  6. 6

    Serve salmon with sautéed spinach and lentils or brown rice.

🌿 Why This Recipe Supports Gut Health

Salmon provides omega-3 fatty acids that support gut lining integrity, while leafy greens and whole grains add fibre and important micronutrients.
